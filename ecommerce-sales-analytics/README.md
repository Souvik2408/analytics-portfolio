
## Business Problem
Analyze year-over-year and monthly performance to understand revenue trends,
profitability, and category-level growth drivers.

## KPIs Analyzed
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Quantity Sold
- YoY Growth
- Monthly Trends
- Market Share by Region

## Key Insights
- LATAM emerged as the fastest-growing market.
- Outdoor and fitness-related categories drove sales growth.
- Seasonal trends highlighted opportunities for targeted campaigns.

## Tools Used
- Tableau
- SQL
- Excel
