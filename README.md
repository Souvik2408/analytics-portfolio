# Analytics Portfolio – Souvik Ghosh

This repository showcases selected Business Intelligence and Analytics projects
focused on real-world business problems using Tableau, Power BI, and SQL.

## Projects

### 1. E-commerce Sales Performance Analytics
Year-over-year and monthly analysis of sales, profit, and quantity to identify
market and category growth drivers.

### 2. Road Safety Analytics Dashboard
Analysis of road accident data to uncover risk patterns across road types,
weather conditions, and vehicle categories.

### 3. Netflix Content Strategy Analytics
Exploratory analysis of Netflix’s content catalog to understand genre mix,
growth trends, and strategic focus areas.

## Tools & Skills
- Tableau
- Power BI
- SQL
- Data Modeling
- KPI & Trend Analysis
