## Business Problem
Analyze Netflix’s content catalog to understand genre distribution, growth
patterns, and strategic focus areas supporting user retention.

## Analysis Focus
- Content mix by genre
- Growth of TV shows vs movies
- Ratings and audience categories
- International vs domestic content

## Key Insights
- TV-MA content dominates the catalog, indicating a shift toward mature audiences.
- Documentaries and stand-up comedy provide high value with lower production cost.
- Rapid content expansion reflects Netflix’s strategy to sustain subscriber engagement.

## Business Impact
Insights help inform content investment decisions and catalog strategy to
balance growth, cost, and audience appeal.
