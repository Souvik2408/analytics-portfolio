## Business Problem
Analyze road accident data to identify high-risk conditions and patterns that
can inform safety measures and policy decisions.

## Analysis Focus
- Weather conditions
- Road types
- Vehicle categories
- Time-based trends

## Key Insights
- A majority of serious accidents occurred on dry roads rather than wet or snowy conditions.
- Single carriageways showed the highest concentration of serious casualties.
- While overall accidents declined, risk patterns shifted based on road type and driving behavior.

## Business Impact
Insights highlight areas where road safety interventions and awareness
campaigns can be most effective.
